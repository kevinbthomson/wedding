Our Story

Our Story began in early 2016 when we met at a dinner party held by our mutual friends, Stephanie and Joey Vernon. Kevin was living in Salt Lake City at the time and was in Denver to visit and ski with the Vernon's. They talked most of the night about their common interests, skiing and running, and eventually went their separate ways. Later that year, while Kevin was visiting again, Jacque was invited to to join everyone for a weekend of spring skiing in Summit County. What was supposed to be a fun day on the slopes turned into a little too much fun at the bar, which was then followed up the next day with a hangover of epic proportions. In an effort to salvage what was left of the ski weekend, Jacque dragged Kevin onto the slopes and they enjoyed an amazing bluebird day at Keystone, curing their hangover with some fresh mountain air.

Once again, they went their separate ways. However, this time with the help of the internet Gods, they were able to reconnect. A few photo likes led to a few messages which led to hours of phone time and eventually a trip to Salk Lake City. Without much convincing, Kevin eventually moved to Denver and it didn't take long to decide that they were going to spend the rest of their lives together.

Colorado.com Guanella Pass

Mount Bierstadt

Hike a 14er! Mt. Bierstadt (14,065 ft) is just 9 miles from the town of Georgetown along the beautiful Guanella Pass. Often considered to be one of the "easier" 14ers, this 7 mile hike is a great option for those coming from sea-level who still want to take on one of Colorado's highest peaks! Don't be fooled by the moderate milage of this hike though, you'll also have a climb of 2,391 feet but the view at the top is worth every step!
